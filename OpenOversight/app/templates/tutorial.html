OpenOversight Labeling Tutorial

We want you to help us go through images submitted by activists and Lucy Parsons Labs' social media monitoring of law enforcement to find faces of police officers and match them to officers in our roster.

Step 1: Find Police Officers

When an image is first displayed, see if you see any faces of police officers. If you do, see if you can make out anything about their badge number or name. If you can even see a few letters or numbers, proceed to Step 2 by clicking "Police officers are clearly visible with badge numbers or names".

If you don't see any clear faces of police officers in the image with names or badge numbers, just click "No police officers with badge numbers or names" to advance to the next image. However, if you do then you will proceed to Step 2.

Step 2: Map Police Faces to Entries in the Roster

We want you to use the cropper to first select an officer face in the image, and then use the search box to try to find them in our roster.

If you can't find them in the roster, click "Can't find this officer".

If you can, select them and click "Add officer face to OpenOversight".

Once you've added every officer from the image, click "I have selected each visible face in this image. Done!". Important: only then will your work be saved, so make sure to click this to save your tags.
